Integrated Cargo System

Introduction

The Integrated Cargo System manages the declaration, profiling and taxation of goods and cargo imported into Australia. It receives over 250,000 documents related to cargo everyday. It consists of over 200 different CICS transactions and batch applications.

System Architecture
